Popular Indian Recipes....!!!

1.Biryani

Biryani is a fragrant rice dish made with basmati rice, meat (usually chicken, mutton, or beef), and spices. The spices include saffron, garam masala, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom, and nutmeg.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken or mutton
  • Garam masala
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Fried onions

2.Paneer Butter Masala

A vegetarian alternative to butter chicken, this dish uses paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a creamy, tomato-based gravy with butter and spices.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (cubed)
  • Butter
  • Tomatoes
  • Cream
  • Garam masala
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)

3.Pani Puri

Small, hollow, crispy puris filled with a spicy, tangy water (pani), along with potato, chickpeas, and tamarind chutney.

Ingredients:

  • Puri shells (semolina flour)
  • Boiled potatoes
  • Chickpeas
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Pani (spicy tamarind water)
